Understanding the Risk

This was a controlled training exercise designed to demonstrate common phishing tactics. By clicking the link, you've helped identify areas where additional security awareness may be beneficial.

Warning Signs to Recognize

  • Unexpected communications about packages, deliveries, or account issues
  • Requests to verify, update, or confirm personal information via email
  • Sender addresses that don't match the organization's official domain
  • Urgent or threatening language designed to prompt immediate action
  • Links that redirect to unfamiliar or suspicious URLs

Best Practices for Email Security

  • Never click links in unexpected or unsolicited emails
  • Verify sender identities through official contact methods
  • Navigate to websites directly rather than through email links
  • Hover over links to preview destinations before clicking
  • Report suspicious emails to your IT security team immediately
